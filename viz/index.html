Fitness flux

Interactive dashboards for two analyses of viral fitness dynamics, built from the Snakemake pipeline's MLR fitness estimates. Each page fetches its data tables at runtime from the analysis results/ directories, so the whole site is static.

Fitness flux →

Scaffolded variant fitness through time, empirical frequencies, mutation–fitness relationships, and the traveling fitness wave (flux vs. variance) for SARS-CoV-2 clades and lineages and H3N2 clades.

Lineage deltas →

Per-branch change in SARS-CoV-2 lineage fitness against mutation counts and mutation-effect predictors (CoVFit, EvEscape, DMS, ESM embeddings), with linear models and predictor correlations.

Ported from Mathematica notebooks to Python + Observable Plot / d3. Source: fitness-flux-analysis/ and lineage-deltas-analysis/.
